FAQ

Who can participate in AGO?

Anyone who is interested in taking part in the competition can do so without any problems; however, if the number of participants is much higher, we will have to restrict the number of participants. In addition, only those who have the role trusted on our server, which will be assigned by the committee, are eligible for the competition.

How to participate in AGO?

The instructions for taking part in the competition are well described in our regulations.

Is bash forbidden in AGO?

The use of bashing techniques is not forbidden, but strongly discouraged, so the use of this technique in solutions can result in a loss of points. For more information please consult the regulations

Is AGO communist?

The AGO is not a political movement and its committee cares little for political ideologies. If you see the symbol of communism on our logo, you'd better check your pareidolia problems.
