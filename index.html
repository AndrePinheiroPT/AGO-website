The Amateur Geometer’s Olympiad (AGO) is an olympiad geometry which aims to discover, encourage and challenge all those people who want to put their knowledge of olympid geometry to the test and promote synthetic geometry in general. The olympiad was founded by André "Atavic" Pinheiro. For more information, join our discord server.

This website, partly developed by the author of the André "Atavic" Pinheiro, was only possible thanks to the open-source repository ipho-unofficial.org on Github and by imo-official.org. The theme and design was copied from imo-official.org
